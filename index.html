about

I study CS at CMU, specializing in Deep Learning and Robotics. Otherwise, I also like systems and theory-heavy math coursework.

My broad interests are robot learning and image/video/action generation models. My current focus is to gain more experience in robot learning and gain more knowledge in NLP areas. I am currently conducting research at CMU Robotics Institute related to real2sim cloth folding.

I wish to connect with you if you share the same interests (I'm active on Twitter)! My 2026 goal is to build a network with enthusiasts in robot learning. Share ideas and learn together - part of why social networks exist.
